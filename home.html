ORGANIZATIONAL FRAMEWORK

Corporate Governance & Operational Strategy

Our operational framework defines how we guide Papua New Guinea's trading network toward long-term institutional execution and capital retention.

Our Mission

To eliminate financial illiteracy in the retail trading sector across Papua New Guinea by providing transparent access to institutional execution systems, backtested risk frameworks, and professional tools without monthly subscription fees.

Our Vision

To establish the premier regulated trading network in the Pacific region, helping local retail traders transition into funded prop firm contractors who protect their capital and manage risk at a bank-grade standard.

Core Operational Values

Absolute Transparency

We reject secret indicators and hidden fees. We explain our broker commission rebate structure openly from day one.

Capital Preservation

Survival takes priority over profit. Our entire architecture is structured to protect account equity first, then capture gains.

Local Empowerment

We optimize our trading layouts specifically for PNG working hours, ensuring local traders can execute perfectly around their jobs.

Core Strategic Objectives

  • 01 / Broker Linkage Validation: To onboarding 100% of our active network under fully regulated partner environments to secure trader liquidity.
  • 02 / Risk Mitigation Control: To mandate strict risk parameters, ensuring no community member ever risks more than 1% to 2% of equity per position.
  • 03 / Prop Firm Capital Funding: To train and guide local PNG traders to comfortably pass institutional evaluations and access massive global capital pools.
